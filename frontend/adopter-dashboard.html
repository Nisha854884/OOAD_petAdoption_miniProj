❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for adoption?

Browse the available pets section, find a pet you'd like to adopt, and click the "Apply for Adoption" button. Fill out your application and submit it. Our team will review your application and contact you.

What documents do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide proof of residence, an ID, and references. Our staff will contact you for more details during the application process.

How long does the approval process take?

Typically, the approval process takes 5-7 business days. You'll receive an email or phone call with the status of your application.

Can I track my application status?

Yes! You can view the status of all your applications in the "My Applications" section of your dashboard.

What if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, we'll provide you with feedback on why. You can improve your application and try again for another pet.

Is there an adoption fee?

Adoption fees vary depending on the pet. Feel free to contact our staff for more information about specific pets.
